Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a topic?

You can add topics directly to your local Shelf by typing, voice dictating (using the microphone icon), or sharing links and text from other apps using the standard iOS Share Sheet. Everything is stored on your device.

When does Return generate a reading?

Generation never happens automatically when you save a topic. A structured reading is only generated when you explicitly select a topic from your Shelf and tap the "Explore" button.

Why did I receive a notification?

Return uses quiet, randomized local notifications to bring saved topics back to your attention. These notifications are scheduled and triggered entirely on your device to help jog your memory and spark curiosity without tracking your habits.

Can I delete saved topics?

Yes. You can delete any topic from your local Shelf at any time. Once deleted, the topic is immediately and permanently removed from your device.

Does Return work without an account?

Yes. Return requires no accounts, profile registrations, or passwords. The app is intentionally designed to be local-first, storing your shelf data directly on-device.

How do I change the app language?

Return is available in English, French, and Spanish. The app automatically respects your iOS device language. You can also customize your language preference specifically for Return in the app settings or via the iOS Settings app under Return.

How do I report a problem?

Please send us an email at support@example.com. To help us solve your issue quickly, please specify which device model, iOS version, and app language you are using.
